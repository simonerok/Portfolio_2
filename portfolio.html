Project 13C.01.01

Choosing a font

Working with fonts a great tool to use is "TypeScale.com". The program allows you to test sizes, font families and pair fonts. Font hiarachy is an important part of a website. We are working with display fonts and paragraph text. Usually iis better to choose a simple dispaly font like a sans serif and not a crazy display font. Choosing fonts its better to go with two types of fonts and work with boldness and making the text itallic etc.

Buttons

I have worked with buttons, a simple look is going to work in most cases because you dont want to confuse the reader. On the contrary buttons are made to draw attention which is why it has to stand out in some way. Thats why colors are important. In my case i have chosen a light color for the button which makes it stand out on the darker background.

hover effect

Hover effect

In addition i have worked with hover effects for the buttons. This is a good way to show the reader that they can interact with the button. Ive chosen a simple hover effect that makes an arrow appear on the button when the mouse is hovered.
